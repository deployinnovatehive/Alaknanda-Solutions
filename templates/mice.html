Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get my loan amount?

Most MICE loans are disbursed within 24-48 hours of approval, with instant loans available within hours for eligible customers.

What documents are required for MICE loans?

Basic documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, and bank statements. Requirements vary by loan type and amount.

Can I prepay my MICE loan?

Yes — most MICE loans allow prepayment without penalties. Check specific terms for your loan type.

What is the minimum credit score required?

MICE loans are designed for various credit profiles. While a good score helps, we consider overall financial health and income stability.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges — all fees including processing fees, interest rates, and EMI amounts are clearly disclosed upfront.
