Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my resume?

You can submit your resume through our online portal or email it to our recruitment team. We'll review it and get back to you within 48 hours.

What types of positions do you recruit for?

We recruit across all levels from entry-level to executive positions, covering various industries including IT, finance, healthcare, manufacturing and more.

Do you provide career counseling services?

Yes — we offer comprehensive career counseling including resume building, interview preparation, career planning and skill development guidance.

How long does the recruitment process take?

The timeline varies by position and complexity, but typically ranges from 2-8 weeks from initial screening to offer acceptance.

Do you work with international candidates?

Yes — we assist with work permits, relocation support and cultural integration for international candidates and assignments.
