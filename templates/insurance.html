Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a claim?

Claims can be initiated via our claims portal or helpline. You will need policy number, incident details and supporting documents. We guide you through the process.

Are pre-existing conditions covered?

Pre-existing conditions may have a waiting period or may be excluded depending on plan terms. Check specific policy wording or ask our advisor.

Can I add riders or add-ons?

Yes — many plans allow optional riders like critical illness, zero depreciation (motor), maternity cover (health) and more.

What documents are required for purchase?

Commonly required documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof (for certain plans), vehicle RC for motor, and medical reports if applicable.

Is there a free-look period?

Most policies offer a short free-look period (usually 15 days) to review the policy terms and cancel for a refund subject to conditions.
